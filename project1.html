Translucent Lambskin Tattooing

Translucent lambskin hide, tattoo ink. Approx 30" x 45".

Second image is the hide on a window, allowing light to pass through.

In this project, I tattooed directly onto translucent lambskin leather to explore tattooing outside of living human flesh. Leather allowed me to study how ink behaves without a body to support the tattoo’s recovery.
